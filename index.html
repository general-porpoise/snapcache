Snapcache

Give physical impact to your digital communications

Context Matters

Snapcache allows you to send rich-media messages that have a physical location in time and space.

Discover Snapcaches

Every time you wander to a new location, you have the chance to discover a cache. One your friends and family left for you to find at just that time, in just that place. Each new location brings a chance to interact with the world both physically and digitally, in a new way.

Share Rich Media

Snapcache content is more than just text. We support image capture, selections from your photo library, and even animated GIFs. Integration with Giphy even allows on-the-fly, unexpected images to be shared using keywords.

Create Physical Impact

Snapcaches are placed in both time and space. When you create a cache, you can control exactly where it is placed, when it becomes available, and exactly how long that cache will exist once it is discovered. Have fun creating exciting scavenger hunts, elaborate traditions, or useful location-based reminders!
